High-Scale Warehouses & Logistics Hubs

Milestone Development constructs durable, scalable warehousing and freight distribution facilities built to support complex industrial machinery and massive structural loads. We evaluate access constraints, gantry crane structural supports, fire safety zoning, and large-clearance loading portals early in the schematic design.

Our structures leverage advanced structural steel framing, composite insulated roof systems, and optimal spacing spans that optimize spatial layout configurations.

Heavy Concrete Slab Slab Engineering

The foundation of industrial operation is the concrete floor plate. We utilize premium slab-on-ground design, joint armor integrations, and post-tensioned configurations designed to eliminate joint failures, concrete dusting, and differential settlements.

Whether storing heavy vehicles, complex bulk handling lines, or automated high-rack storage shelves, we provide slab engineering that meets precise operational requirements.

Industrial Access, Safety & Site Controls

Industrial projects require meticulous attention to heavy vehicle turning paths (B-Double/Semi-Trailers), stormwater management, and active regulatory compliance:

  • Comprehensive Turning Path Swept Path Mapping
  • NCC High-Hazard Fire Containment Systems
  • Durable Concrete Joint Armor Reinforcement
  • Heavy Portal Structural Framing Integrity
